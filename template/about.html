About CineMax

Our Story

Founded in 2010, CineMax has grown from a small movie blog to one of the most comprehensive movie databases on the web. Our mission is to provide movie lovers with accurate information, reviews, and recommendations.

We started with just three passionate movie fans and have grown to a team of over 50 dedicated professionals including film critics, data analysts, and web developers.
